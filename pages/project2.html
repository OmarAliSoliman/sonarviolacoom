Morocco in Abu Dhabi

For the last 4 consecutive years, Viola Communications created a unique event consisting of 3 different parts: the main exhibition and museum in ADNEC, a 2-day concert on the Corniche, and mall stands around the UAE.
The objective was to further strengthen the historically strong ties between the UAE and the Kingdom of Morocco, as well as showcase Moroccan culture to UAE nationals as part of efforts to bring the two nations and people closer together.
