Viola Communications Gets Shortlisted for MEES 2024!

Marketing

Viola Communications is delighted to announce it has been shortlisted for the Middle East Event Awards (MEES) 2024 in the following categories:

- Silver Winner – Best Employer of the Year - Viola Communications - Bronze Winner – Best Social Event - DIFC Sculpture Park Launch - Bronze Winner – Best Use of New Technology at an Event - WAM at GMC - Bronze Winner – Best Event on a Budget - DIFC Sculpture Park Launch - Bronze Winner – Industry Icon - Michail Panagiotakis, Viola Events Director

The Middle East Event Awards is a prestigious platform that recognises excellence within the region's event industry. Its core objective is to acknowledge, honour, and celebrate the achievements of exceptional individuals, teams, and companies who have orchestrated and delivered the most remarkable event experiences throughout the region. This achievement highlights our commitment to staying at the forefront of the industry and rewards our in-built culture of excellence.
