Hay Festival Abu Dhabi 2020

Viola Communications was tasked to introduce the Hay Festival (UK Based), one of the world’s most celebrated literary festivals, to an Abu Dhabi audience and ensure that the first ever collaboration between Ministry of Tolerance (main partner) and Hay Festival UK ran smoothly.

ROI

Reached diverse audience

Garnered attention from Schools

Engaged international literature groups

For the first time in UAE, Viola’s integrated business units excelled at The Hay Festival Abu Dhabi launch, that was showcased in Manarat Al Saadiyat for 4 days, bringing poets, authors and intellectuals to engage, entertain and educate.
