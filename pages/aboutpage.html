Fully Integrated Marcomms Solutions

Part of the Multiply Group, Viola Communications is an industry leader with an extensive network of divisions providing comprehensive marketing, branding, media, and communications solutions.


Our portfolio includes Viola Advertising, Viola Events, Viola Public Relations, Viola Planning & Consultancy, Viola Producers, Viola Outdoor, Viola Interactive, Purple Printing, and Purple Exhibitions.

Diverse and Growing

Established in 2001, Viola Communications has grown into one of the largest integrated communication organizations in the region. Our team of over 300 professionals represents more than 20 nationalities across offices in Abu Dhabi, Dubai, and Cairo.


Strategically headquartered in the UAE capital, our contributions to the communications sector are marked by innovation and client satisfaction.

Committed to Innovation

As a powerhouse in the communications industry, Viola Communications pioneers groundbreaking strategic campaigns and innovative initiatives with government and private entities in the UAE, contributing to the sectors growth.


Our commitment to digital transformation is evident through Viola Outdoor, where we lead with first-of-their-kind digital out-of-home solutions. Our launch of several new DOOH media platforms in Abu Dhabi underscores our commitment to staying ahead of the industry.

Our Clients

At Viola Communications, we specialize in comprehensive marcomms solutions across all business disciplines. Leveraging strategic insights and creative expertise, we deliver integrated projects that resonate with targeted audiences.


We are dedicated to setting new standards and delivering exceptional solutions tailored to our clients' diverse needs. Our client-centric business model ensures seamless experiences for all our stakeholders.
